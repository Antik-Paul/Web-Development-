CP1406/CP5638 - Assessment 2 - Project Plan

Name: Vcare Opticals

Goal

The goal of the website is to provide clear information about eye-care services and make it easier for people to learn about eye health and available treatments. And also to explain the services offered, to provide details about the staffs and also to promote eyewear products. Patients can also easily get an appointment to visit the clinic or to contact with our staffs.

Success Evaluation

The increase of engagements of patients after the launch of the website.

Target Audience

Every people (people of all ages) in the community who requires eye-care services.
