Our Services

We provide a range of professional services to support eye health, vision care, and personalised treatment.

Vision Assessment

A detailed examination of vision clarity, depth perception and color vision to help identify vision changes and support accurate treatments.

Prescription Update

We review and update glasses or contact lens prescriptions to make sure patients continue to enjoy clear and comfortable vision.

Eye-Health Screening

Screenings help check for early signs of eye diseases such as cataracts, glaucoma, and other conditions that may affect long-term eye health.

Retinal Imaging

Advanced retinal imaging allows us to analyse retinal health carefully and support the early detection of hidden problems.

Lifestyle & Visual-Care Discussion

We provide guidance on screen habits, lighting, protective eyewear, and eye-care routines to help patients maintain healthy vision in daily life.

Annual Eye Checkup Package

Our Annual Eye Checkup Package includes the following services:

  • Vision Assessment: A detailed examination of vision clarity, depth perception, and colour vision.
  • Eye-Health Screening: Checks for early signs of eye diseases such as cataracts and glaucoma.
  • Retinal Imaging: Advanced imaging to analyse retinal health.
  • Prescription Update: Reviewing and updating glasses or contact lens prescriptions.
  • Lifestyle & Visual-Care Discussion: Guidance on screen habits, lighting, and eye-care routines.
